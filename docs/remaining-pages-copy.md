## FAQ Section

**Section headline:** Common Questions

---

### How do I request a booking?

Fill out the booking form at lomooutfitting.com/booking. Include your preferred dates, trip type, number of anglers, and whether any anglers are youth. Matt will confirm availability and send a deposit link within one business day.

---

### What does the deposit cover?

The deposit is 80% of the total trip fee, paid at time of booking to hold your date. The remaining 20% is paid to the guide on the day of your trip.

**Deposit amounts by trip type:**

| Trip | Total | Deposit (80%) | Day-of (20%) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Full Day — Single Boat | $675 | $540 | $135 |
| Half Day — Single Boat | $575 | $460 | $115 |
| Early Season Full Day | $500 | $400 | $100 |
| Heroes Program — Full Day | $500 | $400 | $100 |
| Multiple Boats | $675 per boat | $540 per boat | $135 per boat |

---

### What do I need to bring?

The guides provide all fly fishing gear — rods, reels, flies, and terminal tackle. Full day trips include a shoreline lunch and cold drinks. What you need to bring:

- Layers appropriate for the weather. Montana weather changes quickly on the water.
- Hat and polarized sunglasses.
- Sunscreen and bug spray.
- Closed-toe shoes or water shoes. Waders are not required from a drift boat.
- All personal items in one small to medium duffle bag — drift boats have limited storage.

Guides do not provide waders, rain gear, or any clothing. If you need waders, fly shops in Craig and Wolf Creek carry gear and can outfit you before your trip.

---

### Do I need a Montana fishing license?

Yes. All anglers 12 and older are required by Montana law to purchase a fishing license before the trip date. Licenses are purchased through Montana Fish, Wildlife & Parks — not through Lo Mo Outfitting.

Purchase at: fwp.mt.gov/buyandapply/fishinglicenses

License fees are set by the state and are separate from your trip cost.

---

### Where do I stay?

All trips launch from around Craig, Montana. Lodging options:

- **Craig** — Several lodges and cabins on or near the river. Book early, especially for peak season (June–August).
- **Wolf Creek** — 15 minutes south of Craig, additional lodging options.
- **Helena** — 45 minutes south via I-15. Full range of hotels. Good option if you want city amenities.
- **Great Falls** — 55 minutes north. Full range of hotels.
- Tent and RV camping is also available in the area.

Matt will confirm your meeting location before the trip based on where you're staying.

---

### How do I get to Craig?

Craig is on I-15, Exit 234, between Helena and Great Falls. If flying in, Helena Regional Airport (HLN) and Great Falls International Airport (GTF) are both within an hour. Car rental is available at both airports. Matt will confirm the exact meeting location closer to your trip date.

---

### What if I've never fly fished before?

Beginners are welcome. Tell Matt when you book — he'll match you with a guide who enjoys teaching. The Missouri River is a great place to learn. Most first-timers catch fish.

---

### Can I bring my kids?

Children 6 and older are welcome. For safety reasons, children 5 and younger cannot be accommodated on the boats.

Youth anglers 12 and under must be accompanied by a parent or guardian and count as one of the 2 anglers per boat. Per Montana State Boating Regulations (MCA Title 23, Chapter 2, Part 6), children under 12 must wear a U.S. Coast Guard approved life vest at all times on the boat.

---

### What about gratuity?

Gratuity is not included in the trip price and is not required, but it is appreciated. Standard gratuity is $150 for a half day trip and $200 for a full day trip, per guide. For multiple-boat trips, tip each guide individually.

## Policies Section

**Section eyebrow:** Trip Policies

**Section headline:** What to Expect

**Section subhead:** These policies apply to all Lo Mo Outfitting bookings. Read them before submitting a request.

---

### Angler Expectations

All anglers are required to:

- Purchase a valid Montana fishing license for every angler 12 and older before the trip date.
- Dress appropriately for expected weather conditions — including layers, a hat, sunglasses, and closed-toe shoes.
- Be on time to the meeting location confirmed by Matt before the trip.
- Keep all personal items in one small to medium duffle bag. Drift boats have limited storage.

---

### Youth Angler Policy

- Children 5 and younger cannot be accommodated for safety reasons.
- Youth anglers 12 and under must be accompanied by a parent or guardian at all times.
- Youth anglers 12 and under count as one of the 2 anglers per boat.
- Per Montana State Boating Regulations (MCA Title 23, Chapter 2, Part 6), children under 12 must wear a U.S. Coast Guard approved life vest at all times on the boat.

---

### What Guides Provide

**Full day trips:** Fly fishing gear, shoreline lunch, cold soft drinks, shuttle transportation.

**Half day trips:** Fly fishing gear, cold soft drinks, shuttle transportation. No meal is provided on half day trips.

Guides do not provide: waders, rain gear, coats, sunscreen, bug spray, medications, or personal items. These are the responsibility of the angler.

---

### Gratuity

Standard gratuity is $150 per guide for a half day trip and $200 per guide for a full day trip. For multiple-boat trips, tip each guide individually. Gratuity is not included in the trip price and is appreciated but not required.

---

### Cancellation Policy

**14 or more days' notice:** 50% of the deposit is returned.

| Trip | Deposit paid | Refund (50%) |
|---|---|---|
| Full Day Single Boat | $540 | $270 |
| Half Day Single Boat | $460 | $230 |
| Early Season / Heroes | $400 | $200 |

**Less than 14 days' notice:** No refund, unless a detailed explanation is provided. To request a refund, email matt@lomooutfitting.com with the subject line "Refund" before the trip date. All requests are considered case by case and require a conversation with Matt.

**After the trip is completed:** No refunds.

**Weather cancellation:** Weather cancellations are determined by the Outfitter only, with 24–72 hours notice. If a weather cancellation occurs:
- Anglers may roll over to another available date at current rates.
- Anglers may receive a full return of the deposit paid at booking.

> ⚑ **Verify:** Confirm the refund email address. The current site uses `matt@lomoouttfitting.com` (note the typo — double t). Correct address should be `matt@lomooutfitting.com`. All refund requests should go to that address.

---

### Multiple Boat Trip Policies

Multiple boat trips are for parties of 3 or more anglers. Maximum 2 anglers per boat.

- Multiple boat bookings are always full day (7–8 hours).
- Deposit of 80% per boat is due at time of booking to hold all boats.
- Remaining 20% per boat is paid day of trip.

**Example:** A two-boat trip totals $1,350. Deposit at booking: $1,080. Day-of payment: $270.

**Cancellation — multiple boat trips:**

| Boats | Deposit paid | Refund with 14+ days notice |
|---|---|---|
| 2 boats | $1,080 | $675 |
| 3 boats | $1,620 | $1,012.50 |
| 4+ boats | varies | No refund |

Less than 14 days' notice: No refund. See standard cancellation policy above for refund request process.

Weather cancellation terms are identical to single-boat trips.
