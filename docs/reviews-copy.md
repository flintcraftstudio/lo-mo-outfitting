"I had a day on the water with Matt Mohar that left me not only hooked on fly fishing but inspired to pursue guiding myself. By the end of the trip, I wasn't just impressed with his guiding; I wanted to become a guide myself, drawn by his mentorship, enthusiasm, and the way he makes the craft feel accessible and meaningful. ## Implementation Notes

**Avatar images:**
- Use the full-size `geougc-cs` URLs where available — they render at higher quality
  than the `w36-h36` thumbnail URLs.
- Where only the small thumbnail URL exists, display at 48×48px — the image
  is small but sufficient at that size.
- All avatar images should be displayed as circles (`border-radius: 50%`).
- Do not hotlink Google's image URLs in production without confirming they are
  stable. Preferred approach: download each avatar, save to
  `/static/img/reviewers/`, and serve from the Lo Mo domain.

**Guide reviewer callout:**
- Andrew Osborn and Rain Keating are both guides on the Lo Mo roster.
- Their reviews carry extra weight as internal trust signals — a guide
  recommending the outfitter to his own family, and a guide whose experience
  led him to pursue guiding himself.
- Optional: add a small teal badge — "Lo Mo guide" — on their reviewer cards.
  Matt to confirm he's comfortable with this treatment before implementing.

  stale over time. Either display the collected date ("April 2026") or
  pull dynamically from the GBP API once GBP access is established.
